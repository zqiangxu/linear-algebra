# 矩阵的乘法

## 1、矩阵乘法

```math
c
\begin{pmatrix}
y_1 \\
\vdots \\
y_n
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
cx_1 \\
\vdots \\
cx_n
\end{pmatrix}
```


```math
c
\begin{pmatrix}
x_{11} & \cdots & x_{1n} \\
\vdots & & \vdots \\
x_{m1} & \cdots & x_{mn}
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
cx_{11} & \cdots & cx_{1n} \\
\vdots & & \vdots \\
cx_{m1} & \cdots & cx_{mn}
\end{pmatrix}
```
